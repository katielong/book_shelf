This visualization was adapted from N.F.L. draft visualization from N.Y.T. using data from goodreads API. Individual bars were colored by their average ratings on goodreads, and the users can also select books based on the number of pages that each book has. Clicking on each book will lead to the book's page on goodreads. I trimmed down the list to only show top 100, and I am playing with other options to show a more complete picture.
